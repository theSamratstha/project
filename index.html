Samrat Shrestha

Nuwakot, Palpa

I visited the historical and majestic land of Nuwakot. The land that carries the fort of Nuwakot that assisted Jitgadi to forbade the British army to conquer Nepal. The land is home to one the Sen King, King Mukunda Sen. It is filled with diverse cultures and traditions. Not to forget the people here are friendly and kind. The idea of connecting Butwal with Nuwakot is a great idea in my opinion, becuase it would contribute to preserve the history that this land wrote.

Samrat Shrestha

Tansen, Palpa

Tansen is truly magical. I hiked up to Batase Dada, and the experience was incredible. The view from the top was mesmerizing, with the hills stretching endlessly in all directions. After the hike, I indulged in some delicious local foods that made the day even more unforgettable. The combination of nature’s beauty and the rich flavors of the cuisine here is something I’ll cherish forever.

Samrat Shrestha

Tulsipur, Dang

Visiting MamaGhar is always a great time! This trip was extra special because they took me on an expedition to some amazing places. We explored Pandabeshwar Mahadev Mandir, learned about heritage at the Tharu Cultural Museum, and admired the beauty of Purandhara Jharana, among other spots. On top of all the fun, I even earned a total of Rs. 8000 during my time here in Dang—hehe!..

This Dashain was so fun. We (Me and my cousins and sibling) pulled an alnighter to play random fun games and talk about dumb things. We kept laughing for hours. Also not to forget the moments of Tika at every place I visited. Surely this Dashain was fun!
